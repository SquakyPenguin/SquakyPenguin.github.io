about

// the human behind the pen

I'm an innovation design engineer driven to deliver meaningful impact in day-to-day living - focusing on human-computer interaction and behavioural learning to strengthen individual agency.

My background is mechanical: a first-class MEng from Warwick, a placement year designing pharmaceutical automation, and ongoing medical device work. Now at the RCA & Imperial, I'm turning that rigour toward how people learn, focus, and grow alongside technology.

Away from the desk I love to make things with my hands - cosplay builds, crafts, and origami. I also lead the RCA Climbing Society, so I'm probably thinking about this from halfway up a wall.
